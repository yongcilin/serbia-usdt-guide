🇹🇼 繁體中文
你的朋友要用 USDT（一種價值等同美元的數位貨幣）支付給你。只需開設免費的 ECD Wallet 帳戶，就能收款並換成塞爾維亞第納爾（RSD）存入銀行，全程在手機上完成。
1

下載 ECD Wallet App

開啟 iPhone 的 App Store，搜尋「ECD Wallet」，安裝（免費）。需 iOS 17.0 以上。

💡 什麼是 ECD Wallet？ 塞爾維亞本土合法加密貨幣平台，2012 年成立，超過 65,000 位用戶，App 支援塞爾維亞語介面。
2

註冊帳號

  1. 開啟 App，點「Registracija（註冊）」
  2. 輸入你的手機號碼（+381 開頭）
  3. 輸入電子郵件地址
  4. 輸入簡訊和信箱收到的驗證碼
  5. 設定密碼，完成
3

到門市完成身分驗證（KYC）

要把錢提到銀行，需要在附近的合作門市「刷身分證」驗證一次，之後不需要再做。

📍 Niš 的驗證地點（VIP 兌換店）
  • 市中心：VIP Niš–Centar
    Generala Milojka Lešanina br. 2
  • 購物中心：VIP Delta Planet
    Bulevar Nemanjića 11b
✅ 帶塞爾維亞身分證（lična karta）前往
無需預約，現場幾分鐘完成。平日營業至晚間。
4

取得你的收款地址

  1. 在 App 首頁點「Primanje（接收）」
  2. 選擇幣種「USDT」
  3. 選擇網路「TRC20」（手續費最低）
  4. 畫面上出現一串字母+數字，這是你的「收款地址」
  5. 點「複製」，用訊息傳給朋友
💡 這個地址是固定的，可以一直用，傳給朋友一次即可。
5

等待收款

朋友匯出後，通常 5–15 分鐘內 USDT 會出現在你的 App 餘額裡。你會收到通知。

✅ 600 歐元以下的交易，在塞爾維亞無需申報，直接操作即可。
6

把 USDT 換成第納爾（RSD）

  1. 在 App 首頁點「Prodaja（賣出）」
  2. 選擇「USDT」
  3. 輸入要賣出的金額
  4. 確認畫面顯示的即時匯率（依塞爾維亞國家銀行中間價）
  5. 滑動確認，RSD 餘額立刻增加
7

提款到銀行帳戶

  1. 點「Isplata（提現）」
  2. 選擇「RSD 提至銀行」
  3. 輸入你的銀行帳號（第一次需輸入，之後可儲存）
  4. 確認金額，送出
  5. 通常 幾分鐘至數小時內入帳

🇬🇧 English
Your friend wants to pay you in USDT (a digital currency worth 1 US dollar each). Just create a free ECD Wallet account to receive the payment and convert it to Serbian dinars (RSD) directly into your bank — everything done on your phone.
1

Download ECD Wallet

Open the App Store on your iPhone, search for "ECD Wallet", and install it (free). Requires iOS 17.0 or later.

💡 What is ECD Wallet? A legitimate Serbian cryptocurrency platform founded in 2012, with over 65,000 users. The app is available in Serbian language.
2

Create an Account

  1. Open the app, tap "Registracija" (Register)
  2. Enter your phone number (starting with +381)
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Enter the verification codes sent to your phone and email
  5. Set a password — done
3

Verify Your Identity (KYC) at a Store

To withdraw money to your bank, you need to verify your identity once at a partner exchange store. This only needs to be done one time.

📍 Verification locations in Niš (VIP exchange offices)
  • City center: VIP Niš–Centar
    Generala Milojka Lešanina br. 2
  • Shopping center: VIP Delta Planet
    Bulevar Nemanjića 11b
✅ Bring your Serbian ID card (lična karta)
No appointment needed. Takes just a few minutes. Open until evening on weekdays.
4

Get Your Receive Address

  1. On the app home screen, tap "Primanje" (Receive)
  2. Select the currency "USDT"
  3. Choose the network "TRC20" (lowest fees)
  4. A string of letters and numbers appears — this is your wallet address
  5. Tap "Copy" and send it to your friend
💡 This address is permanent — you can reuse it every time, no need to generate a new one.
5

Wait for the Payment

After your friend sends the USDT, it usually arrives in your app balance within 5–15 minutes. You will receive a notification.

✅ Transactions under €1,000 in Serbia do not require reporting. No extra steps needed.
6

Convert USDT to Dinars (RSD)

  1. On the home screen, tap "Prodaja" (Sell)
  2. Select "USDT"
  3. Enter the amount you want to sell
  4. Confirm the live exchange rate shown (based on National Bank of Serbia rate)
  5. Swipe to confirm — your RSD balance increases immediately
7

Withdraw to Your Bank Account

  1. Tap "Isplata" (Withdraw)
  2. Select "Withdraw RSD to bank"
  3. Enter your bank account number (saved after first time)
  4. Confirm the amount and submit
  5. Money usually arrives within a few minutes to a few hours
FAQ
Q: Are there fees?
ECD's selling fee depends on your 90-day trading volume: Casual (new users, under 100K RSD) 4.7%, Active 4.4%, Trust 3.7%. The TRC20 network transfer fee is very low (under $1) and paid by the sender.
Q: Are there transaction limits?
Each sell transaction allows 20–3,000 USDT. €600 is about 650 USDT, so one transaction is enough. Withdrawals under 300,000 RSD arrive within minutes.
Q: Does USDT go up and down in value?
No. USDT is a "stablecoin" — 1 USDT is always worth 1 USD. It doesn't fluctuate like Bitcoin.
Q: Is the platform safe?
ECD is a legally licensed platform recognized by the Serbian government, with 12 years of operation and no security incidents.

Preuzmite ECD Wallet

Otvorite App Store na vašem iPhoneu, potražite "ECD Wallet" i instalirajte besplatnu aplikaciju. Potreban je iOS 17.0 ili noviji.

Registrujte nalog

  1. Otvorite aplikaciju, tapnite "Registracija"
  2. Unesite broj telefona (počinje sa +381)

  4. Unesite verifikacione kodove primljene na telefon i email
  5. Podesite lozinku — gotovo
3

Verifikacija identiteta (KYC) u poslovnici

Da biste mogli da podignete novac na bankovni račun, potrebno je jednom da verifikujete identitet u partnerskoj menjačnici. Ovo se radi samo jednom.

4

Pronađite svoju adresu za primanje

  1. Na početnom ekranu tapnite "Primanje"
  2. Izaberite valutu "USDT"
  3. Izaberite mrežu "TRC20" (najniže naknade)
  4. Pojaviće se niz slova i brojeva — to je vaša adresa novčanika

5

Sačekajte uplatu

Nakon što prijatelj pošalje USDT, obično za 5–15 minuta sredstva će se pojaviti u vašem saldu. Dobićete obaveštenje.

✅ Za iznose ispod 1.000 EUR u Srbiji nema obaveze prijave. Nema dodatnih koraka.
6

Pretvorite USDT u dinare (RSD)

  1. Na početnom ekranu tapnite "Prodaja"
  2. Izaberite "USDT"
  3. Unesite iznos koji želite da prodate
  4. Potvrdite prikazani kurs (prema međukursu Narodne banke Srbije)
  5. Prevucite za potvrdu — RSD saldo se odmah povećava
7

Podignite novac na bankovni račun

  1. Tapnite "Isplata"
  2. Izaberite "Isplata RSD na bankovni račun"
  3. Unesite broj bankovnog računa (čuva se posle prvog puta)
  4. Potvrdite iznos i pošaljite
  5. Novac obično stiže za nekoliko minuta do nekoliko sati
